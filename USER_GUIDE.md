# 📖 Richmond Storyline Generator - User Guide

Welcome to the Richmond Storyline Generator! This guide will help you create compelling stories that capture the essence of Richmond.

## 🌐 Getting Started

### **Set Up the Application Locally**

1. **Clone and set up the project** (see main README.md for details)
2. **Start the application**:
   ```bash
   docker-compose up -d
   # or
   python integrated_app.py --environment development
   ```
3. **Access the application** at **http://localhost:3000**

### **Create Your Account**
1. Click the **"Sign Up"** button in the top right
2. Enter your email address
3. Create a secure password
4. Complete your profile with your Richmond connection

### **Demo Account** (Optional)
You can also use our demo account to explore features:
- **Email**: demo@localhost
- **Password**: RichmondStories2024!

## 🎤 Creating Your First Story

### **Method 1: Voice Recording (Recommended)**

1. **Click the Microphone Button** 🎤
   - Allow microphone access when prompted
   - You'll see a beautiful waveform visualization

2. **Record Your Story Idea** (30-60 seconds)
   - Speak clearly about your story concept
   - Example: *"I want to tell a story about how Richmond's downtown has transformed over the past decade, focusing on the mix of historic preservation and modern innovation."*

3. **Review the Transcription**
   - Our AI will transcribe your audio automatically
   - You can edit the transcription if needed

### **Method 2: Text Input**

1. **Click "Start with Text"**
2. **Type Your Story Idea**
   - Be as detailed or brief as you like
   - Include any specific Richmond connections

## 💬 The Conversation Phase

After providing your initial idea, our AI will engage you in a conversation to develop your story:

### **What to Expect**
- **Depth Analysis**: The AI analyzes your idea across 4 dimensions
- **Clarifying Questions**: You'll be asked to add personal details
- **Angle Selection**: Choose the perspective for your story
- **Tone Selection**: Pick the voice and style
- **Length Selection**: Choose from short post to full article

### **Sample Conversation Flow**

**AI**: *"This is a fascinating concept! What's a specific moment when you personally witnessed this transformation?"*

**You**: *"Last month I saw a startup demo in a building that used to be a tobacco warehouse."*

**AI**: *"Perfect! That contrast is powerful. What angles interest you most?"*
- 🏗️ **Historical Transformation** - Focus on before/after
- 💼 **Economic Impact** - Emphasize business growth
- 👥 **Human Stories** - Center on people's experiences

### **Tips for Great Conversations**
- ✅ **Be specific** - Mention dates, places, people
- ✅ **Add personal connection** - Why does this story matter to you?
- ✅ **Include sensory details** - What did you see, hear, feel?
- ✅ **Think about impact** - Who benefits from this story?

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### **Common Issues**

#### **Microphone Not Working**
- Check browser permissions for microphone access
- Ensure no other apps are using the microphone
- Try refreshing the page and allowing access again

#### **Story Generation Taking Too Long**
- Our AI typically responds within 10-30 seconds
- If it takes longer, check your internet connection
- Try refreshing and starting over if needed

#### **Can't Edit My Story**
- Make sure you're logged into the correct account
- Check if the story is in "published" status (create a new version to edit)
- Try clearing your browser cache

#### **Export Not Working**
- Ensure pop-ups are allowed in your browser
- Check your download folder for the file
- Try a different export format

### **Browser Support**
- ✅ **Chrome** (recommended)
- ✅ **Safari** (iOS and macOS)
- ✅ **Firefox**
- ✅ **Edge**
- ❌ Internet Explorer (not supported)

## 💡 Tips for Better Stories

### **Before You Start**
- 🎯 **Know your audience** - Who will read this?
- 🏙️ **Connect to Richmond** - What makes this uniquely local?
- 📅 **Consider timing** - Is this tied to current events?

### **During the Conversation**
- 💬 **Be conversational** - Imagine talking to a friend
- 🔍 **Add specifics** - Names, places, dates matter
- 💖 **Include emotion** - How did this make you feel?

### **When Editing**
- 📖 **Read aloud** - Does it flow naturally?
- ✂️ **Cut ruthlessly** - Remove unnecessary words
- 🎨 **Show, don't tell** - Use vivid descriptions

### **Richmond-Specific Tips**
- 🏛️ **Use local landmarks** - Reference places people know
- 👥 **Include diverse voices** - Richmond is beautifully diverse
- 📚 **Respect history** - Acknowledge the city's complex past
- 🌉 **Bridge communities** - Connect different parts of Richmond
